Coolors Warm-up

Warm-up: Color Scheme Creation

Follow these instructions to create a custom color scheme.

  1. Go to coolors.co
  2. Accept cookies and close out of the initial pop-up(s)
  3. Click the "Start the generator!" button
  4. Close out of the pop-up
  5. Press the space bar to randomize the color scheme
  6. Press the "lock" button on a color you like to hold it
  7. Continue to press space and lock colors you like
  8. Remove colors by click the "x" button
  9. When you have a scheme you like, click the "Export" button
  10. In the pop-up, click the "Copy URL" button
  11. Paste the URL to share it!
